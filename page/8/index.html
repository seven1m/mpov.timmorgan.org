Code Snippet Comments RSS Feed

Aug 23, 2006
In my quest to make everything into a feed… I present the Code Snippet Comments feed. It goes with this site, which lacks a feed for comments posted on snippets. For this feed generation, I used a free service called Feed43. It’s quite powerful! Basically, you give it a URL and about ten minutes of your life working out some pattern recognition, and voila! you get a feed auto generated every six hours based on the content of the page.
